About us

2005

Our story begins years ago, when with little funds and big desire, we finally decided to start putting our ideas into reality. We proudly can say we are the fisrt ever architecture studio in Albania to offer our clients all kinds of architectural and design sevices. From a group of 4, we are now reaching 50 employees working together to deliver to you comfort and beauty.

What we do?

We are The Future Studio architects, a full-service design and consulting firm specializing in forensic architecture, quality assurance, and expert witness testimony. We ensure sound design and legal compliance throughout a building's lifespan, from cradle to grave.

5-Year Warranty

We are so confident in our work that we offer an unprecedented 5-year warranty on all workmanship, and full assistance with all manufacturer warranties.

Our Approach

The happiness of our customers is our singular goal. Creating joy in our customers’ experience is the byproduct of combining our superior building systems with a rugged moral code emphasizing honesty and fairness.

The team

With over 15 years experience, our staff offers a unique blend of expertise as designers and consultants. We’ve been told this combination of talents uniquely supports our clients’ needs, but we believe our hands on experience and knowledge of building systems are the foundation for the specialized services we offer.

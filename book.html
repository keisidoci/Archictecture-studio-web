FACADE DESIGN

Our facade design process provides a tailored service to every client, from concept inception through to operational use. Independent, technical advice and robust engineered solutions are delivered to provide clarity to architectural designs, and therefore confidence in project costs. Key to this process is applying existing knowledge and further research into materials, facade systems and manufacturing techniques to suit the client’s expectations while complimenting the architect’s vision. After facade construction, we remain involved in the compliance monitoring on site, with periodic site visits and reporting to the Client team or to the main contractor.

OFFICE DESIGN

Founded to provide world class working environments with an emphasis on quality, innovation, technical design and unparalleled delivery and service that is aiming to foster agility and empower technology in today working environment. We transforme a partially below-grade expansion space into a dynamic, welcoming place where people want to be. The elegant design celebrates the company’s culture. We bring natural light into the open work area and glass meeting rooms through the high windows on one side and the sky-lit atrium on the other.

INTERIOR DESIGN

The interior is an integral part of the design. When designing a building it is impossible not to think of its interior. Therefore, the third pillar of our business is interior design. Our advantage is a full support of design - from architectural concept, through detailed design, author's supervision, to advice on selecting and purchasing equipment. We recommend verified contractors. We focus on simplicity, great natural materials, good ornaments and the highest quality equipment.
